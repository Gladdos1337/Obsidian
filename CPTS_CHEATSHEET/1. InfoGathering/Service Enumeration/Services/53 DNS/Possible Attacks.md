## 1. DNS Spoofing / Cache Poisoning

- **Mechanism:** Injecting fraudulent DNS records into a resolver's cache.
## 2. DNS Tunneling

- **Mechanism:** Encapsulating data/protocols inside legitimate DNS queries (like `TXT` or `CNAME` records) to bypass firewalls.
## 3. Domain Hijacking (via Subdomain Takeover)

- **Mechanism:** Claiming a legitimate subdomain that points to a defunct external service.
